Faculty Advisor

Alison Norman

Dr. Norman is a lecturer in The University of Texas at Austin Computer Science Department. As an Associate Professor of Instruction, Dr. Norman teaches systems to undergraduate students. Her courses include Principles of Computer Systems and Ethical Foundations of Computer Science. She is interested in the the intersection of supercomputing and program analysis, static techniques to improve checkpointing, and large-scale simulation.
Dr. Norman is incredibly passionate about nurturing the next generation of women STEM leaders and does outreach to underserved communities.
